ABOUT BEST FRIENDS

Our Difference

Best Friends is the country's first and longest running Play & Stay pet destination, with more than 40 locations nationwide (and growing), including Walt Disney World Resort.

We offer an array of best-in-class pet care services all under one roof. Our core services are Boarding (for the furry and feathered alike), Doggy Day Camp, and Grooming. We also offer Puppy Play Group, Training, Vet Clinics, and Transport services at select locations.

Our mission from day one was to raise the bar for pet care, and we take pride in being industry innovators to this day. From the moment a pet walks through our doors, we get to know them as a unique individual and welcome them into our tight-knit circle of friends.
